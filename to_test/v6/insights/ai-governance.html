AI Governance for Critical Infrastructure — Govantis Insights_H1

By Augusto Silva & Pedro Caldeira dos Santos

Deploying AI in telecom environments where failure is not an option requires a fundamentally different approach to governance. Traditional AI ethics frameworks were not designed for production systems that carry live traffic, manage critical infrastructure, and operate under regulatory scrutiny.

This whitepaper proposes a governance framework purpose-built for AI in telecom: model validation pipelines, continuous monitoring, regulatory alignment (EU AI Act, GDPR), and incident response protocols for AI systems in production.

Topics: Model risk assessment, AI audit trails, explainability in network operations, regulatory compliance for AI/ML systems, and building operator trust in autonomous decision-making.
