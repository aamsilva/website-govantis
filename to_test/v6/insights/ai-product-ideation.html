AI Product Ideation at Scale — Govantis Insights_H1

By Augusto Silva & Pedro Caldeira dos Santos

The traditional telecom product development cycle — concept, requirements, prototype, validate — takes months. AI-native product ideation compresses this to days. Not by cutting corners, but by using AI as a force multiplier across every stage of the ideation process.

This whitepaper presents a practical methodology for telecom product teams: how to use AI for rapid prototyping, how to validate AI-generated concepts against real operator constraints, and how to build a repeatable ideation pipeline that produces production-ready concepts at scale.

Topics: AI-assisted prototyping, concept validation frameworks, from ideation to MVP, building repeatable AI pipelines, and case studies in telecom product innovation.
