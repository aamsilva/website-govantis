# DESIGN.md - GOVANTIS Design System

## Brand Identity
**GOVANTIS** - Corporate Solidity (Telecom) + Technological Disruption (AI).

## Visual Aesthetic
- **Vibe:** B2B High-end, Minimalist, Precision-oriented (similar to Linear/Stripe).
- **Key Characteristics:** Large whitespace, subtle gradients, crisp borders, and refined typography.

## Color Palette
### Primary (Trust & Governance)
- `Slate 950`: `#020617` (Deep Background)
- `Slate 900`: `#0f172a` (Card Background)
- `Slate 400`: `#94a3b8` (Secondary Text)
- `White`: `#ffffff` (Primary Text)

### Accents (AI & Innovation)
- `Electric Blue`: `#3b82f6` (Primary Action)
- `Cyan 400`: `#22d3ee` (Gradient Highlight)
- `Teal 500`: `#14b8a6` (Success/Innovation)

## Typography
- **Scale:** Geometric Sans-Serif (Inter / System Sans)
- **Heading 1:** `text-5xl font-extrabold tracking-tight`
- **Heading 2:** `text-3xl font-bold tracking-tight`
- **Body:** `text-base leading-relaxed`

## UI Tokens
- **Border Radius:** `rounded-lg` (8px), `rounded-full` (999px)
- **Shadows:** Subtle elevation using `shadow-xl` with low opacity.
- **Borders:** `border-slate-800` for subtle separation.
- **Transitions:** `duration-300 ease-in-out`

## Components
- **Buttons:**
    - Primary: Electric Blue gradient with white text.
    - Secondary: Slate 800 background, Slate 200 text, subtle border.
- **Cards:** Slate 900 background, 1px Slate 800 border, slight hover lift.
- **Glassmorphism:** Background blur `backdrop-blur-md` for navigation and overlays.
